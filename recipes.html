#Seafood Aglio Olio

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain and transfer to a pasta bowl

2. Combine garlic and olive oil in a cold skillet. Cook over medium heat to slowly toast garlic, about 10 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low when olive oil begins to bubble. Cook and stir until garlic is golden brown, about another 5 minutes.

3. Stir red pepper flakes, black pepper, and salt into the pan.

4. Pour in the pasta into the pan\, and sprinkle on oregano leaves and stir until combined.

5. And ready to serve!

#Scalloped Potatoes

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a casserole dish

2. Heat the pan, put ¼ of butter, one table spoon of flour, a bit of salt and pepper and lastly pour one cup of milk.

3. Layer the bottom the casserole dish with potatoes. followed by a light dusting of flour, salt and pepper. Pour milk over the entire casserole.

4. Cover the potatoes with a layer of cheese and coriander.

Bake it in oven, and ready to serve!

#Beef Onion Ring Burger

1. In a bowl, mix together the ground beef, salt, pepper, and onion powder

2. Peel and slice the onion into 1-centimeter (½ inch) rings. Detach the rings from each other

3. Take a spoonful of the beef mixture and press it into the bottom of a ring

4. Push a cheddar square into the center of the beef

5. Seal the top with another spoonful of the beef, pressing it flat

6. Repeat with the rest of the rings

7. Place the flour, eggs, and bread crumbs into three separate bowls

8. Dip a ring into the flour, shaking off excess, then into the egg, the bread crumbs, then back to the eggs, and one last time into the breadcrumbs

9. Fry the rings for 5-6 minutes, until deep golden-brown

10. Drain on a paper towel, then ready to serve!
